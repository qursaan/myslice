Automatic vs. Manual delegation of credentials

Automatic delegation of credentials

Don't delete private key, ease-of-use option:

  • This means that you will keep your private key in the portal and it will automatically delegate your credentials on your behalf.
  • This option does not provide as high of a level of security; however, your private key will still only be used in the portal and will be available to you and the OneLab admins only.

Manual delegation of credentials

Download & Delete private key, higher security option:

  • If you delete your private key, you will have more security but the portal will no longer be able to delegate credentials on your behalf, and you will have to manually delegate your credentials to the portal.
