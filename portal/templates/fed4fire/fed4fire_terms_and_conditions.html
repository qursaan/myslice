Context

Fed4FIRE provides an experimental platform for testing new ideas and technologies in the area of computer networking (http://www.fed4fire.eu). The federated facility consists of individual testbeds and tools, with different owners. The Fed4FIRE consortium is coordinated by iMinds.

Fed4FIRE provides You with basic access (through a single account, common tools, support and documentation) to the Platform, while each individual testbed provider determines the specifics of this access in terms of available resources, access policies, etc.
